# [[Advanced Generative AI Workflows and Prompt Engineering Principles]]

## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> True mastery of generative AI lies not in knowing the latest tool, but in mastering structured prompting techniques—defining roles, setting constraints, and providing detailed inputs to guide the output toward a specific narrative or aesthetic goal.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **Workflow Structuring:** The most effective process involves pre-planning (e.g., using Gemini for storyboards) before generating final assets (e.g., feeding storyboard content into Sora).
- **Prompt Depth and Constraint:** Professional results require highly structured prompts that define roles, enforce visual constraints (e.g., 5x4 grid, pure white background), and specify operational rules.
- **Consistency Management:** For multi-asset generation (emoticons, character arcs), the prompt must explicitly mandate consistency in style, character appearance, and tone across all outputs.
- **Understanding Tool Limitations:** Success depends on understanding the 'grammar' of the tool—knowing which inputs guide or confuse the AI to achieve a desired emotional or structural effect.
