# [[End-to-End AI Book Creation and Market Validation Pipeline]]

## 📌 한 줄 통찰 (The Karpathy Summary)
> This integrated system automates book publishing by first validating market demand via competitive analysis, then generating a structured blueprint, and finally drafting publishable chapters.

## 📖 구조화된 지식 (Synthesized Content)
- **Phase 1: Market Validation (Keyword & Competition Analysis):** Determines commercial viability by analyzing Amazon KDP data (Title/Subtitle, Category, Price, Review Sentiment) to generate a 'Publishability Score' and competitive intensity index.
- **Phase 2: Book Blueprint Generation:** Designs the book architecture based on validated keywords and selected format (How-to/Guide), utilizing best-seller chapter patterns and SEO principles to define goals and structure.
- **Phase 3: Chapter Drafting Engine:** Creates full, publishable drafts using a defined tone and reading level, applying fixed templates (Introduction, Core Content, Checklist) while removing redundant phrasing.
